Workshops and Exhibitions

Events such as gallery exhibitions and workshops are listed below.

Workshops

Hands-on opportunities to learn gumoil and gravure printing vary from year to year, country to country. Enrollment for each workshop is limited to small groups and can be custom-arranged. I also give gumoil workshops from time to time at my Albuquerque studio. These take at least three days, usually consecutive.  Contact me if you or your organization wants to arrange a workshop.
